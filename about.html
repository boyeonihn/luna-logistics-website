Our Story

Luna Logistics was founded in 2018 by Alton and Vonnae' Henry to honor “Luna”, Alton's late mother, a hard-working woman from Kingston, Jamaica. Alton and Vonnae' wanted to continue her legacy and show their daughter the importance of hard work, and that a woman could do anything she put her mind to. “Success is not something that is easily attained, but something possible when you're determined”, are the words they feed their daughter, and how they were each raised.

Luna Logistics began as a freight company where we specialized in transportation of cargo and various goods in the North and Southeast regions of the country. As we've seen success in these regions, we've scaled our business and strategy to expanded into all regions domestically. We've grown into a company that provides Business Consultation, Computer Maintenance and Repairs, Office Moving, Janitorial Services, and Extermination and Pest Control.

In our expansion, we've been disciplined in ensuring the highest level of customer satisfaction by ensuring quality of quantity; reliable execution; and being thoughtful in timeliness of services to cut cost.
